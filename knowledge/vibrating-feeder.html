Vibrating Feeder

Vibrating feeder is one of the crusher equipment frequently used in industry, vibrating feeder can be within the prescribed period of time the material flowing from the storage bunker, even to the crusher. Often used with vibration conveying sieve, bucket elevator, and other supporting, not only the production process automation, but also save the cost, improve the yield, it is dressing, metallurgy, mine, abrasive and other industries the best feeding equipment.

Vibrating feeder timing, uniform, continuous feeding characteristics to avoid crushing equipment idle, stuffy car situation ,to ensure the crushing machine can work normally and the crushing efficiency and yield. After years of development, there are many kinds of vibration feeder, in accordance with the mode of motion working member can be divided into straight, reciprocating, and rotary. Screw feeder and impeller feeder belong to rotary, vibration feeder belongs to reciprocating. In view of the feeder many models, we should consider from many aspects at the choice of feeder. For example, the feeding quantity, adjusting range, installation, environment and their own should weight into consideration.

On the mechanism of sand production line, vibration feeder in addition to have the function of the feeding also have the function of the screen, vibration feeder can be non-stone material screened out. Vibration feeder has a lot of advantages, such as simple structure, abrasion resistance, convenient to repair.

Vibrating feeder in sand industry to realize automation, full motion is not to be ignored, and now, the country is in rapid development period, companies are also followed by rapid development. In recent years, our country stepped up to the high-speed rail, power generation, hydraulic industry, urban construction investment, which means that the demand for sand and gravel aggregate are also growing, It has also led to the rise of around sand factory, a large amount of sand and gravel aggregate a lot of demand at the same time, the production of stone and sand equipment is the key.

Natural sand have already can't satisfy the demand of the market, need a lot of artificial sand and gravel aggregate construction. With the continuous development of aggregate investment, the sand and gravel equipment requirements have also increased, especially the highway, high-speed rail, such as advanced engineering, is very strict with sand equipment and stone production equipment.

Shibang machine constantly attracts foreign senior talent, the production of independent innovation of vibrating feeder is mainly used for sand production line and stone production line equipment, has good performance and reliability, also has advantages of structure design.
